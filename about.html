About Me

My life in a nutshell: I was born and raised in West Bloomfield, Michigan in a family of five, one of which is my identical twin sister. Having a twin has played perhaps the most influential role in my life and is something that I have learned to truly appreciate. My family is extremely important to me, and I am constantly thankful for all that they have done for me. Also, my friends are a large network of support that I always turn to when needed, which I am grateful of. When it comes to passions, I am fascinated by the ways in which people are impacted by social media platforms and the information surrounding them through the means of our digital world. On a lighter note, I am passionate about health and wellness. Maintaining a consistent and beneficial daily routine, involving clean eating and exercise, has kept me at a healthy state, both physically and mentally. Furthermore, I am a self-proclaimed foodie and have co-run a food Instagram blog with my identical twin for many years (@twinstalktaste). I am always on the lookout for delicious new meals or cool restaurants to try!

Skills: I am a very hardworking and determined person in all facets of life. I always strive to achieve the goals that I set for myself, and persevere through anything that gets in the way. I have very strong communication skills, both verbal and written, which I find to be very important as we transition into an increasingly digital world. To continue, I am a skilled writer, having held leadership positions as Editor-in-Chief and Editorial Director of two organizations. I have published multiple articles and really enjoy writing. Referring to my more technical skills, I am proficient in Python and am currently learning HTML and CSS. With this in mind, I am always interested in expanding these skills and learning other languages, as well. I ensure that I remain open-minded, inclusive, and critical of all the work that I produce, always looking for ways to improve what I have previously created. With the above being said, I am very curious, always seeking to learn more about a variety of subjects, and enjoy gaining new skills.
